All-Guitar Strategy

1. Create Value - All-Guitar creates incredible value by offering industry unification--a unique service. Further value is created by offering affordable and effective promotional services to brands.

2. Establish Authority - All-Guitar establishes authority by serving as an accurate, comprehensive, and trustworthy directory for guitar-related brands. It aspires to be an authoritative "single source of truth" for a highly-fragmented industry.

3. Drive Traffic - All-Guitar drives significant traffic through a "freemium" business model which offers free basic listings to all brands. Brand listing information is also offered free to consumers, dealers, and distributors which supports brand research and purchasing decisions.

4. Serve Customers - All-Guitar partners with brands by helping them boost visibility, telling their stories, categorizing their offerings, and spotlighting their strengths. We work closely with customers to identity needs and engineer solutions.

5. Generate Systems - All-Guitar generates systems that make it easy for brands to claim, submit, and maintain their listings. These systems are also vital to long-term sustainability.  Intuitive, well-tested systems foster efficiencies, repeat business, and revenue.

6. Leverage Strengths - Unique Dataset • Industry Unification • Domain Authority • Freemium Model • Mobile-First • Global Footprint
